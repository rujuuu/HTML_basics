Staring at the Sink

Rujuu

I don’t know what to feel,
I am tired yet hopeful,
Driven yet so out of sync,
Filled with nothing but regret.

As I sat and ponder what’s going on in my head,
I accepted the fact there was nothing to read.
Nothing was here, and nothing will fill.
Monsters lurk, they won’t stay still.

In this vast and open world with endless possibilities,
I am nothing but a mere speck of dust.
It’s not even enough to even cause any allergies,
I am just here, and here I am to last.

Like a train, I long for a track;
With no clear path ahead, I feel lost.
I am lucky to have people behind my back—
I am so out of breath, I feel lost.
